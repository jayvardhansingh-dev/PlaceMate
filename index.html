Welcome to PlaceMate

PlaceMate is a Placement Management System that helps students explore job opportunities, apply for placements, and connect with top companies through one platform.

About Project

The Placement Management System is designed to simplify campus recruitment by connecting students with recruiters. Students can view companies, apply for jobs, track applications, and manage their placement activities through an easy-to-use web portal.
